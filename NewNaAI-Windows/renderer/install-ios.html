Install NewNa.AI on Your iPhone

Requirements: iOS 12.2 or later, Safari browser
1Open in Safari

Make sure you're using Safari browser on your iPhone. This won't work in Chrome or other browsers.

2Tap the Share Button
⬆️

Tap the share button at the bottom of Safari (the square with an arrow pointing up).

3Add to Home Screen

Scroll down in the share menu and tap "Add to Home Screen".

4Name Your App

You can keep the default name "NewNa.AI" or change it. Then tap "Add" in the top right corner.

5Launch the App

Find the NewNa.AI icon on your home screen and tap it to launch the app!

Note: The app will work offline for basic features after the first load. For chatting with AI models, you'll need an internet connection to your Ollama server.
